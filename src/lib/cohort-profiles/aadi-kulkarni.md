Aadi currently works on the international policy team of Coinbase, a leading blockchain and crypto company. He’s interested in the way governments globally build policy frameworks around emerging technology.

Longer term (and at Newspeak House) he’s interested in the way tech and law infrastructure need updating to facilitate public service delivery.

He’s worked in AI ethics, policy, and law research at Cornell University and Microsoft Research, founded a machine learning startup that summarized academic research to the 8th grade reading level and has worked in roles in the US public sector.
