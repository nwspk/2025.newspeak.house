---
title: Huda Abdirahim
---

Huda is a software engineer working on digital asset custody, and co-founder of TreasureCorp, a real-time treasury analytics platform for decentralised autonomous organisations. Her interests lie at the intersection of collective finance, political infrastructure, and programmable governance.

She’s particularly curious about how decentralised communities make decisions, allocate funds, and build legitimacy—especially when contrasted with traditional institutions. Treasure emerged from this tension: a tool that brings budget transparency, proposal tracking, and financial accountability to DAOs, drawing on ideas from civic tech and public finance.

Beyond her work, Huda contributes to open-source, volunteers at Ethereum hackathons, and supports women in tech through internal workshops. She’s interested in how code shapes power—who sets the rules, how they’re enforced, and what transparency means in digital systems.

At Newspeak House, she’s keen to explore how political technology can support new forms of governance—on-chain, off-chain, and everything in between.
